What is SIS ?

NS 051 J,E SENIOR INTEGRATING SEMINAR, 2 units, autumn
A study of the characteristics and interrelationships of the different branches of the natural sciences and mathematics, and their relation to other disciplines. Small group study of important books on these topics, special lectures and general discussions attended by all students enrolled. Required of all Natural Science majors in the senior year.

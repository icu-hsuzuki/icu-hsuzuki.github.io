Grading Policy for AY 2006

毎週、小テストを行ない、レポート問題を出すこともあります。評価は、出席点を含めた小テスなどの合計点（50%）と、学期末試験（50%）で行います。詳細はクラスでも説明します。（2006.9.5）

The grade is determined by T = S+F which is computed by the following formula. If the square root of 50 x T is at least 60, Grade D or above is guaranteed. The grade distribution will be curved so that the overall course GPA would be in between 2.5 and 2.7.

  1. S = MIN(100, Q + P + E).
    1. Q = the total of 8 quizzes.
    2. P = MAX(0, 3 x N - 4), where
      N = MIN(8, the number of quizzes submitted + 1 (if the survey test was taken)).
    3. E = MIN(10, 3 x the number of challenge problems submitted and solved correctly).
  2. F: The points obtained in Final.

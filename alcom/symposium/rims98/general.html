RIMS Symposium on Algebraic Combinatorics 1998

General Information

RIMS98 HOME
Symposium on Algebraic Combinatorics will be held at Research Institute for Mathematical Sciences, Kyoto University (RIMS).

  • Date : December 14, 1998 - December 16, 1998
  • Organizer : Hiroshi Suzuki, International Christian University

  • Proceedings will be published as a volume of "RIMS Kokyuroku" in 1999.

Announcements

December 9, 1998

Both of the talks of originally announced invited speakers from abroad have been cancelled because of their personal but unexpected problems. We are very sorry for it. However, Professor Paul Terwilliger of University of Wisconsin cordially accepted our urgent invitation and he will be giving a talk on Tuesday morning on his recent develepement in the study of Lenard system.

Organizer: Hiroshi Suzuki
